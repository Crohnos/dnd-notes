# Elemental Plane of Fire

> Inner Plane - Fire

## Overview
Endless expanse of flame and heat. Home to efreet, fire elementals, and salamanders.

## Description
Sea of fire with islands of solid flame and brass. Air itself burns.

## Notable Features
- City of Brass (efreet capital)
- Ever-burning flames
- Extreme heat damage
