# Feywild

> Plane of Faerie

## Overview
Vibrant echo of the Material Plane where magic runs wild and emotions are heightened. Home to fey creatures and stolen children.

## Description
An enchanted reflection where colors are more vivid, magic is stronger, and time flows strangely.

## Campaign Connections
- **[[landmark-the-first-tear]]**: Major portal in Feywild Borderlands
- Denisse's stolen child lives here
- Source of Feywild Guards (animal races)

## Notable Features
- Time flows differently
- Emotions intensified
- Wild magic common
